Tuan Mai

Learn all about me!

Ever since I was little, I have always had a passion for Engineering, whether it be software or hardware. I always found myself trying to tinker with technology, and just about anything mechanical and complex. Wondering how the parts worked together, reverse engineering, tearing it apart and rebuilding just to understand its process so that I can get a grasp and use that knowledge to build something of my own.

I brought that passion with me through my schooling, taking any engineering related courses, and excelling in them. I also have a great passion or trait of constantly needing to learn new things, and excelling in as much as I can. This brings up a past memory of receiving a failing grade in my Grade 10 Software Engineering course, with a written comment from the teacher that wrote: "Tuan should never pursue programming as a career." But I never let that grade nor comment bring down my passion for computer science / software engineering as I am now a recent graduate of the Honours Bachelor Degree of Computer Science.

I love coding and now have great experience as a mobile application developer and as a researcher. With lots of knowledge in cross domain fields of health and retail management. I am constantly seeking opportunities to create a means to help society through software and new technology. Basing my undergraduate thesis on developing a wearable augmented reality guidance system to read DICOM medical data and present a more efficient and effective procedural planning system for medical professionals.

I hope to find a place that I can help leave a trail to empower and change the world for the better. My specialties include quickly learning new skills and programming languages, problem solving and analysis, reliable and organized. I have experience with C#, C++, Swift, Java, Javascript, Python, HTML5, CSS3, SQL, and Git. I am still enthusiastically trying to learn new languages, frameworks, or principles to increase my knowledge and skills.
